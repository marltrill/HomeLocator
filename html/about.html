A Student Project




What is HomeLocator?

  • Your ideal neighborhood locator based on your personal preferences.
  • Web application to be used in the search for your new home.
  • Finds the most suitable neighborhoods for you.
  • Adjust results to your specific criteria.

What makes HomeLocator different?

  1. No hidden agenda.
    • Not financed by realtors or financial institutions.
    • Not trying to make a profit on hidden charges.
    • No user payment.
  2. HomeLocator offers a visualization of areas fitted to the users demands.
    • As opposed to some other sites where a specific address must be provided in order to see results.
